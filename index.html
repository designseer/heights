Precision Craftsmanship for Every Metal Work

These services are delivered with precision and expertise to meet a wide range of industry needs.

Metal Sheet
Perforation Services

These services involve the process of punching or drilling holes into metal sheets to create patterns, shapes, or designs. Here we use specialized machinery and techniques to achieve precise perforations in size, shape, and spacing.

Laser
Cutting

Our Laser Jet services provide precise cutting solutions for a wide range of materials, ensuring accuracy and efficiency in every project. With advanced technology, we deliver high-quality cuts tailored to your exact specifications.

Water Jet
Cutting

Here we offer versatile and precise cutting for a variety of materials, including metals, without heat distortion. This method ensures clean, accurate cuts, making it ideal for intricate designs and complex projects as well.

Welding
Services

Heights Engineering offer a range of welding services, including Arc, TIG, MIG, and Aluminum welding, to meet the diverse requirements. Our expertise ensures precision and durability across various materials and applications.
